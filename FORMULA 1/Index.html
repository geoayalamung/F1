What is Formula One?


Formula 1® racing began in 1950 and is the world’s most prestigious motor racing competition, as well as the world’s most popular annual sporting series:The 2019 FIA Formula One World Championship™ runs from March to December and spans 21 races in 21 countries across four continents. One World Championship Limited is part of Formula 1® and holds the exclusive commercial rights to the FIA Formula One World Championship™.
